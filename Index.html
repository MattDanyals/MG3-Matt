Protecting Your Investment

Regular maintenance is vital for any vehicle, but for a sophisticated Hybrid+ system like the one in your MG3, it's the key to unlocking maximum efficiency, reliability, and long-term battery health. This guide outlines the recommended service schedule to keep your MG3 performing at its peak, from the first check-up to 200,000 kilometers and beyond. Following this schedule ensures both the petrol engine and the advanced electric components work in perfect harmony.

Core Maintenance Schedule

First 1,000 - 5,000 km

Initial Inspection

  • General safety and systems check.
  • Check and top-up all fluid levels.
  • Inspect for any potential fluid leaks.
  • Confirm proper operation of all lights and electronics.

24,000 km or 12 Months

Minor Service

  • Replace engine oil and filter.
  • Comprehensive vehicle health check.
  • Inspect tyre condition and pressures.
  • Check brake system components.
  • Inspect and clean 12V battery terminals.

48,000 km or 24 Months

Intermediate Service

  • All items from 24,000 km service.
  • Replace brake fluid.
  • Replace cabin air filter (pollen filter).
  • Inspect suspension and steering components.

72,000 km or 36 Months

Minor Service

  • Repeats the 24,000 km service schedule.
  • Replace engine oil and filter.
  • Comprehensive vehicle health check.
  • Deep inspection of braking system wear.

96,000 km or 48 Months

Major Service

  • All items from 48,000 km service.
  • Replace engine air filter.
  • Replace spark plugs.
  • Inspect auxiliary drive belts.

160,000 km or 10 Years

Long-Life Service

  • This interval may vary, consult your dealer.
  • Replace engine coolant.
  • Replace hybrid inverter coolant.
  • Thorough inspection of all hoses and cooling system components.

Note on Service Cycles

The maintenance cycle repeats. For example, the 120,000 km service is similar to the 24,000 km service, and the 144,000 km service mirrors the 48,000 km one. Always perform the tasks for the specified mileage or time, whichever comes first.

Key Component Lifespan

Beyond scheduled services, certain components wear out over time. Here are typical replacement intervals to help you budget and plan ahead. Actual lifespan depends on driving style and conditions.

Tyres

Inspect regularly. Replace every 40,000 - 60,000 km.

Brake Pads & Discs

Regenerative braking often extends life. Inspect annually, replace as needed (typically 60,000 - 100,000+ km).

12V Battery

Replace every 3 - 5 years.

Wiper Blades

Replace every 6 - 12 months for clear visibility.

Detailed Component Maintenance Breakdown

This section provides a comprehensive list of key components in your MG3 Hybrid, detailing their typical maintenance tasks and recommended intervals. Keep in mind that actual intervals can vary based on driving conditions and individual vehicle usage.

Engine Oil & Filter

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 10,000-15,000 km or 12 months

Brake Fluid

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 48,000 km or 24 months

Cabin Air Filter (Pollen)

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 48,000 km or 24 months

Engine Air Filter

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 96,000 km or 48 months (or more frequently in dusty conditions)

Spark Plugs

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 96,000 km or 48 months

Engine Coolant

Task: Replace

Interval: 160,000 km or 10 years

Hybrid Inverter Coolant

Task: Replace

Interval: 160,000 km or 10 years (or 120,000-200,000 km / 5-10 years for other hybrids)

Tyres

Task: Inspect, Rotate, Replace

Interval: Rotate every 8,000-11,000 km; Replace every 40,000-60,000 km

Brake Pads & Discs

Task: Inspect, Clean, Lubricate, Replace

Interval: Inspect annually; Replace as needed (60,000-100,000+ km)

12V Battery

Task: Inspect, Replace

Interval: Replace every 3-5 years (or proactively due to anecdotal reports)

Wiper Blades

Task: Replace

Interval: Every 6-12 months

High Voltage Battery

Task: Health Monitoring (Diagnostic)

Interval: Designed for 8-15 years or 160,000-320,000 km

Inverter System

Task: Inspection, Software Updates

Interval: Annual/Regular Service, As needed for updates

e-CVT Transmission Fluid

Task: Drain & Refill

Interval: 48,000-145,000 km or 2-4 years (check manual)

Shock Absorbers/Struts

Task: Inspect, Replace

Interval: Inspect regularly; Replace 80,000-160,000 km

Suspension Bushings

Task: Inspect, Replace

Interval: Inspect regularly; Replace 80,000-240,000 km

Control Arms/Ball Joints

Task: Inspect, Replace

Interval: Inspect regularly; Replace 112,000-240,000 km

Fuel Filter

Task: Replace

Interval: 48,000-96,000 km (check manual)

Wheel Alignment

Task: Check & Adjust

Interval: Every 2-3 years, or with new tires/suspension work

Rust Protection

Task: Inspect, Apply Aftermarket Coating

Interval: Proactive inspection annually; Coating as needed (especially in harsh climates)
